Header

Note, header tags are based on hierarchy not the look of the size. Typically your h1 will match your title. Keep it consistent.

You should never skip header tag numbers e.g., go from h1 to h4. There are instances where ou will break this rule occasionally, but know why you do it.

Header tags go from h1-h6. IT is rare that you wil use h5 or h6.

Paragraphs

This is a paragraph tag. Paragraphs will make up the bulk of your text.
